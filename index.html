Event Shows

The most famous in Saudi Arabia is the Najd parade, in which Arab warriors mainly participate in the Najd region in central Saudi Arabia before facing their jihad in battle. The sword dance was a way to display the men's weapons and fighting spirit. The Najdi Games, which were led by the founder of Saudi Arabia, King Abdulaziz Al Saud, are also on display. Today, this folkloric dance is performed in all Saudi governorates and has become a symbol of traditional Saudi culture.
